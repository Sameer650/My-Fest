MY FEST-2020

MY FEST 2020 is organised by MITS WEB DEVELOPMENT CLUB,MITS COLLEGE,ANGALLU.
This fest is celebrated since 2016.Many youth participate in this fest to showcase their talents.
There are many technical events,cultural events and sports conducted.
For participating in the events,students will receive a certificate.
Students who won in the competitions like dance,singing,quiz,sports,etc., will receive prizes like I-Phone,One-Plus,Rs.10000/-etc.
